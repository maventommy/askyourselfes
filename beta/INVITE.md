# Ask Yourselves — early beta invite

You are one of 10 early testers for Ask Yourselves: an app where you talk to your
future self, in your own aged face and voice. Very early beta, 18+.

**Open on your phone:** https://ask-yourselves.pages.dev

- **iPhone:** open in Safari, tap the Share button, then "Add to Home Screen" so it
  opens like a real app. Allow the microphone when it asks.
- **Android:** open the link, tap the install prompt if it appears, allow the microphone.

**Try this:** answer the questions, add a selfie, meet your older self, then talk to it.
You can type, or tap the mic and speak. Tap "hear it" on a reply to hear it out loud.

Tell me anything that felt off, broken, or moving: tommy@mavenagency.io

(Heads up: it is anonymous for now, so if you clear your browser or switch phones,
your future self starts fresh. That is on the roadmap.)
